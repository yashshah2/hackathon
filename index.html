A person named “Y” is working for a company called “A”. He gets a new job at company “B”. Once person “Y” gets a job at company “B” he submits his documents to the HR of company “B” digitally. HR of company “B” has to validate the documents submitted by “Y” with his previous companies. Design a platform wherein

1. A HR of a company can edit/add an employee’s details when he is leaving a company.

2. A HR of a company can validate the details of an employee with the previous companies where employee was previously working.

Made with by Neeraj Deshpande, Pavan Maller, Rohan Kosandal, Yash Shah.
